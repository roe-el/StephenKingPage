Stephen E. King

Creator of Worlds

American author of horror, supernatural fiction, suspense, science fiction, and fantasy.

"Books are a uniquely portable magic."
ABOUT

Stephen King was born on September 21, 1947 in Portland, Maine. He and his brother were raised by their single mom after their father left when King was two years old. He fell in love with writing after finding The Lurker in the Shadows by H.P. Lovecraft in his attic, a book that had once belonged to his father. Since then he has written more then 200 short stories and his books have sold over more than 350 million copies. He has received numerous awards for his works and several of his stories have become major motion pictures.

  • Carrie(1973)

    King's first novel about a teenage girl with psychic powers. He threw his early draft in the trash but his wife fished it out and encouraged him to finish it. It has become two feature films.

  • The Shining(1977)

    A story about an aspiring alcoholic writer who becomes the caretaker of a massive hotel in the Colorado Rockies. Supernatural forces push the writer to insanity. The novel was adapted into a film in 1980 directed by Stanley Kubrick.
